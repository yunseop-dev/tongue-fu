The teenage boy next door is a hip-hop fanatic. I went to bed at 11pm and the sound of hip-hop music was too loud. Go home immediately and ring the doorbell to say, 'Turn off the music right now!' I want to say What should I do?

  • Run like a child and run to the neighbor to argue.

    • Do you know what time it is now?
    • If you don't keep your son quiet right now, I'll do it myself!

  • grumbling

    • Who can gain peace and stability by listening to such trashy music?

  • threatening

    • Turn it off now or I'll call the police!

  • think about whether to choose a battle or not.

    • Is this a trivial thing? Does it last? I don't know if it's trivial, but you're going through the same thing three days a week.

  • Evaluate again whether it is worth a head-to-head confrontation.

    • Intentional or unconscious? Is it possible to change? What's the situation? Is it a good time to talk?

  • Decide to talk

    • Could you tell your son to turn down the music?

You're working at a restaurant. A customer beckons to you during a busy evening and complains like this. 'Hey, waiter. I ordered it because I thought it would be fresh because it was a special menu. But the salmon is still frozen. It's a salmon that's been out of the water for a long time." How would you answer that?

  • Explain why

    • Fresh salmon was supposed to come in today, but there was no product in the market, so I used frozen salmon.

  • Extend the argument.

    • The chef said it's frozen, but it's very fresh. There's almost no difference in taste.

  • Avoid responsibility.

    • It's not my fault. If there were no ingredients, the manager had to adjust the menu on his own.

  • Think about whether the customer's words are true, and if so, I admit it.

    • You're right. The menu says fresh salmon.

  • Apologize and try to end the quarrel.

    • I'm sorry I couldn't give you the dish you were looking forward to. I'll change it to another menu you want.

  • convey one's thanks

    • Thank you for saying this. We want everyone who visits the restaurant to be satisfied with the food and visit again.
